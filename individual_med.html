Napa 500 Tablet (500mg)

Manufacturer: Beximco Pharmaceuticals Ltd.

Generic: Paracetamol

Type: Pain reliever and fever reducer

Price: ৳10.80 (per strip)

Used to treat mild to moderate pain and fever. Safe for most patients under doctor's guidance.

Medicine Overview

Napa 500 is used to relieve pain and reduce fever. Common uses include headache, body ache, toothache, and common cold. It is often prescribed by doctors for these conditions.

Uses of Napa 500

  • Pain relief
  • Fever

Side Effects

  • No common side effects

How to Use

Take 500mg medicine as advised by your doctor. Swallow it whole. Napa 500 should be taken with food.

How it Works

Paracetamol in Napa 500 blocks pain signals and reduces fever by influencing the body's temperature regulation system.

Dosage & Administration

Adult Dose

1-2 tablets every 4-6 hours. Maximum 8 tablets per day.

Child Dose

For children (6-12 years): 1/2 to 1 tablet 3-4 times daily.

Precautions

Do not use if you are allergic to paracetamol. Consult your doctor if you have liver disease or alcohol use problems.
